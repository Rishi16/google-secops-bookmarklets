How to install

1
Show your bookmarks bar

Press Ctrl + Shift + B to toggle it on if it's hidden.

2
Drag a button into the bar

Click and hold either bookmarklet button above, drag it up to your bookmarks bar, then release.

3
Open your SecOps SOAR Search page

Navigate to your SecOps SOAR Search page as normal.

4
Click the bookmark to activate

Filters are applied automatically, the tab is renamed, and the page refreshes every 5 seconds.

5
Pauses when a row is selected

The refresh pauses automatically while you have a case row selected so it doesn't interrupt your work.

6
Click again to stop

Clicking the bookmark a second time stops the auto-refresh and the badge turns orange.

Badge reference

Live Unassigned Cases

Static label shown on the search bar for the Unassigned queue view.

Live SIR Cases

Static label shown on the search bar for the SIR incident response view.

Refresh ON

Auto-refresh is active and clicking Apply every 5 seconds.

Refresh Paused

A table row is selected — refresh is paused to avoid disruption.

Refresh OFF

Bookmarklet was clicked a second time and auto-refresh has stopped.

NO APPLY BTN

Apply button not found on page — retrying every 2 seconds.

⚠️ Other is running

A toast appears at the bottom of the page if you try to start while the other bookmarklet is already active.
